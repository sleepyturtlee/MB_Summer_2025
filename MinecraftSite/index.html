Best Minecraft Biomes!!

Cherry Grove

  • Rarity: Uncommon (but biome-specific)
  • Unique Mobs: Bees, Foxes, Rabbits
  • Unique Plants: Cherry Trees, Pink Petals, Bee Nests

An area where cherry trees generated. They are easy to distinguish by their pink leave blocks that have petals that fall to the ground.

Lush Caves

  • Rarity: Common, but may be difficult to find
  • Unique Mobs: Axolotls, Glow Squids
  • Unique Plants: Moss, Azaleas, Spore Blossoms, Dripleaf, Glow Berries

Caverns filled with moss, bushes, vines, glow berries, dripleaf plants, and other vegetation. Axolotls and tropical fish can appear here. These caves are always underground below azalea trees.

Flower Forest

  • Rarity: Rare
  • Unique Mobs: Bees, Rabbits, wolves
  • Unique Plants: All Flowers, Bee Nests, Oak Trees

Less trees appear in these forests, but are replaced with tons of flowers, some exclusive to these biomes. Rabbits replace wolves in these areas.

Bamboo Jungle

  • Rarity: Very Rare
  • Unique Mobs: Pandas, Parrots, Ocelots
  • Unique Plants: Bamboo, Jungle Trees, Melons

A jungle absolutely covered in large trees and thickets of bamboo. Pandas spawn in this area more often.

Swamp

  • Rarity: Common
  • Unique Mobs: Slime, Witch, Frog
  • Unique Plants: Blue Orchid, Lily Pad, Vines, Mangrove trees

A dark hued area with shallow waters scattered around. Trees can sometimes grow out of the water and vines drape down them. Temperate frogs, blue orchids, and swamp huts appear here.

Warm Ocean

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Unique Mobs: Tropical Fish, Pufferfish, Dolphin
  • Coral, Sea Picles, Seagrass

Looks vibrant. These biomes can naturally generate coral reefs and where you can find dolphins, pufferfish, and tropical fish.
